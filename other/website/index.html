Website:
Created for the Web Fundamentals class while working on my BSSD degree @WGU. This website is a depiction of my favorite hobbies reaching as far back as my early childhood when I first helped my friend copy the code for a game out of a magazine to play on his Commodore 64 computer.

Games:
I have always enjoyed games with family and friends, but even from an early age I thought there was nothing better than computer games. For me those games have been nearly exclusively on the PC. Over the years I have had several different consoles but always seem to gravitate back the PC game world. I occasionally still play board games with family and friends but nothing beats the immersion I get from the PC.

Books:
I wasn't always a reader, I did the occasional book report in school if I had to but never read on my own. I was nearly 24 before I picked up a book on my own and even then it was only to calm my nerves while flying for business trips. Apparently my hate for books was trumped my fear of falling, from the sky!

TV Shows:
Thanks to Netflix, Hulu and Amazon we have a wide variety to watch and access to all pass seasons, so that even if you just found a new favorite you wont miss a thing. After watching many shows I have decidedly become a huge fan of British based programs. I have seen a few of those shows redone by U.S. companies but they don't compare to the original and just become sad knock offs.
